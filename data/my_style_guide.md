# Pinterest Style Guide -- Joyce Benson

## Tone & Voice

-   Friendly, encouraging, and helpful.
-   Simple, clear explanations.
-   Mix of Kenyan + international examples.
-   Motivational tone when teaching Pinterest strategies.

## Visual Style

-   Clean, minimalist designs.
-   Pastel colors: beige, blush pink, soft blue, muted green.
-   Bold readable fonts:
    -   Titles: Montserrat / Bebas Neue
    -   Body text: Lato / Open Sans
-   Use high-quality lifestyle images.

## Pin Structure

-   Clear headline (4--7 words).
-   Small sub-text for extra detail.
-   Clean background, no clutter.
-   Strong contrast between text and images.

## Content Preferences

-   Niches: Beauty, Home Decor, Fitness, Money Tips, Travel, Fashion,
    Motivational Quotes.
-   Audience: Women aged 18--45 (Kenya + International).

## Branding

-   Keep text alignment consistent.
-   Add subtle borders or drop shadows.
-   Avoid excessive emojis.
